Game Mechanics for now:


The battle has two Players
Each Player has a list of Monsters
Each Monster has a list of Attacks
Each Attack has a type, damage probablity, and hit probablity
The players select which attack they want to use
The battle lasts until one player runs out of monsters
A winner is declared
